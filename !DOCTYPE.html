About Us

Konnectus is a marketing company that helps new and existing businesses with limited funds to reach their target audience and grow their brand. We believe that every business deserves a chance to succeed, regardless of their budget or size.
We started Konnectus in 2023, after noticing a gap in the market for affordable and effective marketing solutions. We realized that many small businesses struggle to compete with larger and more established competitors, who have access to more resources and expertise. We wanted to change that.
We offer a range of services, including web design, social media management, content creation, SEO, email marketing, and more. We tailor our strategies to suit each client’s needs and goals. We work closely with our clients to understand their vision, values, and customers.
We have helped hundreds of businesses across various industries and niches to increase their online presence, generate more leads, and boost their sales. Some of our success stories include:
Green Planet, a sustainable fashion brand that increased its organic traffic by 300% in six months.
Fit Zone, a fitness app that gained over 10,000 downloads and 5-star reviews in three months.
Bake It, a bakery that doubled its revenue and expanded to two new locations in one year.
We are passionate about what we do, and we are always looking for new challenges and opportunities. If you are looking for a marketing partner that can help you grow your business without breaking the bank, look no further than Konnectus. Contact us today for a free consultation and quote.
